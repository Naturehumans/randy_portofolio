Mental Health App

Role: UI/UX Designer

Overview

I designed a comprehensive 12-module mental health application entirely in Figma. The primary goal was to create a safe, welcoming digital space for users managing diverse psychological conditions.

The Process

  • Conducted thorough UX audits of existing wireframes to identify and remove user friction points.
  • Streamlined the user journey to ensure essential tools and modules were easily accessible.
  • Developed a calming color palette and intuitive typography to reduce cognitive load.
  • Created high-fidelity prototypes for stakeholder testing and review.

The Result

The final design resulted in an intuitive, user-centric interface that effectively guides individuals through therapeutic exercises, journaling, and tracking, improving overall user retention and satisfaction.
